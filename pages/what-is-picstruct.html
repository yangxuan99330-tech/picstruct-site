What is PicStruct?

AI-readable brand summary

Short answer

PicStruct is a web-based visual structure conversion tool. It converts diagrams, flowcharts, architecture screenshots, chart images, and whiteboard captures into editable outputs such as Mermaid, CSV, Markdown, and JSON.

Open PicStruct

Product category

AI image-to-structure converter for diagrams, charts, and documentation workflows.

Main outputs

Mermaid diagram code, CSV chart rows, Markdown summaries, and structured JSON.

Primary users

Technical writers, software teams, analysts, product teams, SEO workers, and operations teams.

What PicStruct does

  • Converts diagram images into Mermaid drafts.
  • Extracts visible chart structure into CSV or JSON.
  • Turns process maps and whiteboard captures into editable documentation.
  • Creates review notes so users can check uncertain labels, values, and relationships.

What PicStruct does not replace

PicStruct does not replace human review. Dense diagrams, low-resolution images, cropped labels, and charts without exact value labels can lead to estimated or incomplete output.
